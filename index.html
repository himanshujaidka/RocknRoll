Journey from ice-cream shop to own manufacturing

Rock n Roll ice-cream company is owned and founded by Mr. Bhagwan Dass Kataria which is situated in Lane Number 11, Gandhi Colony, Muzaffarnagar, Uttar Pradesh (251001). After lot of hardwork and dedication of Mr. Bhagwan Dass Kataria, Mr. Bharat Kataria (CEO), and Mr. Lokesh Kataria (Manager) company gained his popularity in Uttar-Pradesh.
